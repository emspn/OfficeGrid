# Privacy Policy for OfficeGrid

**Last Updated: January 23, 2026**

OfficeGrid ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how your information is collected, used, and safeguarded when you use the OfficeGrid mobile application.

## 1. Information We Collect

### A. Personal Data
*   **Email Address:** Collected during signup/login for authentication purposes via Supabase Auth.
*   **User ID:** A unique identifier assigned to your account to manage your tasks and preferences.

### B. App Data
*   **Task Data:** Titles, descriptions, statuses, and remarks added to tasks.
*   **Team Data:** Employee names and roles within your company workspace.
*   **Analytics:** Aggregated metrics regarding task completion and team productivity (Admin only).

## 2. How We Use Your Information
We use the collected data solely to provide the core functionality of the app, including:
*   Authenticating your access.
*   Managing and assigning tasks.
*   Providing real-time alerts and notifications.
*   Generating productivity insights for administrators.

## 3. Data Storage and Security
*   **Storage:** Your data is stored securely using Supabase (PostgreSQL).
*   **Encryption:** All data is encrypted in transit using HTTPS and at rest within the database.
*   **Isolation:** We use Row Level Security (RLS) to ensure that users can only access data they are authorized to see.

## 4. Third-Party Sharing
*   We **do not** share, sell, or rent your data to third parties.
*   There are **no advertisements** or third-party tracking scripts in this application.

## 5. User Rights and Deletion
*   **Access and Update:** You can view and update your profile and task information within the app.
*   **Data Deletion:** Users can delete their tasks and remarks. To request full account deletion, please contact your company administrator or our support email.

## 6. Children's Privacy
OfficeGrid is intended for business use and is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

## 7. Changes to This Policy
We may update this policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the "Last Updated" date at the top of this document.
